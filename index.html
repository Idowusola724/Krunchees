About Me

Hi, I'm Adeleye Idowu Olorunobanisola, the founder of Krunchees! My love for traditional Nigerian snacks inspired me to start this business. Growing up, I enjoyed homemade treats like chinchin, kulikuli, and plantain chips, and I wanted to share that joy with others. Krunchees was born out of a passion for bringing authentic, crunchy goodness to snack lovers everywhere.

At Krunchees, we believe in quality, taste, and tradition. Our snacks are made with love, using only the best ingredients to ensure every bite is delicious and satisfying.
